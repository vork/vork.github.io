Conclusion

Conclusion
  • 3D Gen AI is a hard problem
  • Even more so with a focus on games
  • Bringing in techniques from inverse rendering is a promising direction

  • SV3D propose to leverage existing video diffusion models
  • This can help overcome data scarcity
  • Reconstruction from consistent multi view generations is easily possible
  • However, it is time consuming (10 min)

  • SF3D propose a feed-forward method which can generate assets in 0.5s
  • We explicitly tackle delighting and material generation
  • Additionally, we propose fast UV unwrapping and texturing
