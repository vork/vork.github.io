Mark Boss

Ph.D. Student

University of Tübingen

I am a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Hendrik P. A. Lensch in the Computer Graphics Group at the University of Tübingen. My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and computer graphics. The main research question is how to perform inverse rendering on sparse and casual captured images. Here, I primarily focus on enabling efficient material appearance acquisition.
